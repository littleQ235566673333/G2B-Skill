These data are collected along with those on short-term balances but are not incorporated in the regular monthly series on capital movements. Table 2 appeared for the first time in the December 1949 issue of the "Treasury Bulletin", giving data for the end of the calendar years 1942 through 1948. Corresponding data will be published for subsequent years.
