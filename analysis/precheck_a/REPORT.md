## Analysis (informational)

### Why mode-selector might track multi-seed mean more closely

| | SS | WTQ |
|---|---|---|
| historical multi-seed mean | 69 | 82.7 |
| today's original single-seed | 66 (-3) | 86 (+3) |
| today's mode-selector single-seed | 67 | 84 |

Both mode-selector results are **closer to historical multi-seed mean than today's originals**. Two interpretations:

1. **Variance reduction**: structured format reduces output variance without shifting mean. Plausible if mode-selection forces the model into one of 4 fixed cognitive frames, reducing free-form drift across runs.
2. **Coincidence**: with 4 single-seed numbers, the appearance of "regression toward known mean" can arise from random sampling.

To distinguish, would need 3+ seed multi-seed eval per variant ($30-50). Per `feedback_effect_first_paper_second_stop_drifting`: not worth it. Mean did not move; that's the question we asked.

### Cross-link to memory

This result confirms `feedback_discrete_retrieval_backbone_split` was the right lens for PTB termination but does NOT generalize to mode-selector. Mode-selector's executor-side selection is in the "continuous-modify-behavior" class, but here it produced no mean improvement. So:
- continuous-modify-behavior is the right *class* (doesn't fail the way discrete retrieval did)
- but format-only restructuring within v8's already-trained skill content does NOT push GPT-5.4 past parity

The remaining hypothesis space: **content-level mechanisms** (different skill content, different training signal) rather than format-level reorganization.

## What this rules out / leaves open

**Rules out:** "v8's skill content is sufficient; only the format limits GPT-5.4 — restructure to amplify it." Mean did not move. Even if variance shrank, that's not paper-grade.

**Does not rule out:**
- Mode-diverse training (K=4 with explicit mode advantage signal during training, not just at execution time). This was Step B in the original plan; we never reached it.
- Other content-level interventions (richer evidence pool during patching, different diagnose strategy, etc.).
